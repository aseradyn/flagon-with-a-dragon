# Country Style Ribs

I've been very into braises recently, and this is a grand one! When done, the meat is fork tender and full of flavor. I don't think it even needs BBQ sauce for serving.

Adapted from 'Best Damned Slow Cooker Ribs' from recipeteacher.com

## Ingredients
### Rub
* 2 Tbsp brown sugar
* 2 tsp paprika
* 2 tsp salt
* 2 tsp onion powder
* 2 tsp garlic powder
* 1 tsp ground mustard
* 1 tsp black pepper

### Braise
* 2 lbs boneless pork ribs
* 3/4 cup chicken broth
* 1 Tbsp lemon juice or apple cider vinegar
* 1 Tbsp soy sauce or Worcestershire sauce
* 1 tsp liquid smoke (optional)

## Instructions
Mix rub, then coat the ribs. Rest for 1 hour.

Preheat oven to 325.

Slice an onion and put the slices in the bottom of a Dutch oven.
Lay the ribs on top, and pour the broth, lemon juice, soy sauce, and liquid smoke over the meat

Bake for about 4 hours. Should be falling apart.
