# Enchiladas a la Craig

This is classic college dude food - open a few cans, a little stirring and baking, and out comes something delicious that a foodie would turn their nose up at.

Eat at your own risk

## Ingredients

1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 can Old El Paso green enchilada sauce
1 can Rotel tomatoes
1 box frozen taquitos
Shredded cheese

## Instructions

Stir together the cream of mushroom soup, enchilada sauce, and tomatoes. Cook until hot through and bubbling.

Arrange taquitos on the bottom of a casserole dish or 13x9 baking pan. Pour the sauce over the top, then a generous layer of shredded cheese.

Bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes.
