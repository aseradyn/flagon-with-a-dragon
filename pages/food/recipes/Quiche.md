# Quiche

Love a good crustless quiche. Tasty, but easy on my blood sugar for those times when I'm really fight it!

## Ingredients

* 2 eggs
* 4 Tbsp heavy cream
* 1/4 tsp salt
* Dash of pepper
* 1/2 cup shredded cheese
* Whatever fillings you want, pre-cooked

## Instructions

Whisk eggs and cream together, then stir in remaining ingredients.

Bake in a large ramekin for 25 minutes at 375
